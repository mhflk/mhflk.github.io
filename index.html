About the MHFLK

MHFLK stands for Mental Health Foundation in Sri Lanka,If you want to learn anything about Counselling Psychology and Psychological Treatments this is the best place which is providing guidelines by a Srilankan State University Professor (Prof. Gnanadasa Perera),Courses are at reasonable price including training periods and practical sessions.We offer basic and advance courses on Psychology with many other courses that will help you to find a place in Sri Lankan Psychological community.

We are providing free and paid lectures on Psychology islandwide and organize events that will improve Psychological knowladge of people from all around the world.

Mental Health Foundation is the best place where you can get both practical and theory sessions in psychology for very reasonable price in Sri Lanka,for more informations you can visit to our about page.
